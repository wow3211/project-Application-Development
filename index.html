Explore Diriyah

Historical Facts: Diriyah is the ancestral heart of the nation, serving as the capital of the First Saudi State and home to the UNESCO-listed At-Turaif district.

Ticket Information

  • General Entry: Free on weekdays until 4 PM.
  • Diriyah Pass: 100 SAR (Redeemable on food/beverage).

Explore AlUla

Historical Facts: AlUla acts as a window into deep antiquity, famous for the monumental tombs of Hegra carved directly into sandstone cliffs by the Nabataeans.

Ticket Information

  • Standard Tour: 95 SAR per person.
  • Old Town: Free entry (Reservation required).
